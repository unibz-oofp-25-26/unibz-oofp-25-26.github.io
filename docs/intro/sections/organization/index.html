    Organization #

    The course consists of:

    • 20 lectures (40 hours), and
    • 10 labs (20 hours).

    Julien Corman will teach the lectures and labs.

    Schedule #

    Lectures #

    By default, lectures are scheduled on:

    • Tuesday 15:30 - 17:30
    • Thursday 10:30 - 12:30

    Hint. Lectures can be followed via Teams (e.g. for students sitting at the back of the class), by joining the corresponding meeting (in the “Lectures” channel of this course’s Team).

    Labs #

    By default, labs are scheduled on:

    • Thursday 15:30 - 17:30

    One lab will be dedicated to a mock exam, and another to its correction.
    The other labs will be dedicated to the assignments for this course.

    Timetable #

    Warning. Some weeks will see fewer lectures/labs, and schedules or room numbers may vary from one week to the other.
    So make sure to check the timetable.

    Announcements #

    All announcements will be made via the “General” channel of the Team for this course.

    These include:

    • release of an assignment,
    • correction of an assignment (and the associated registration form),
    • last-minute cancellations,
    • etc.
