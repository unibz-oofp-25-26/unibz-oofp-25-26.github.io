    Office hours #

    You can ask for office hours to get help with:

    • prerequisites for this course,
    • an assignment,
    • a notion seen during a lecture,
    • etc.

    These appointments must be scheduled via email or Teams.

    Additional content #

    Office hours can also be organized on demand for a group of students, on any of the following topics:

    • hierarchical file system,
    • git,
    • using a terminal,
    • installing/updating/deleting software on Windows, macOS and Linux,
    • text editors and IDEs (landscape and usage).

    This content is optional. It is meant to help you interact with a computer, so that your learning experience does not get impaired by technological obstacles.

    Further details will be provided during the first lab (dedicated to software-related questions for this course).
